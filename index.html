MaskUp

Propaganda meets pop-art.

Mask Up in style

Breathing is one of the few things that everyone on the planet does, all the time. But breathing is also something we know very little about. Mask Up makes thoughtful goods, without exploiting people or the planet. MaskUp is not about tradition. You can’t pass it on.

Now available in whatever you want.

AIRINUM TECH

When you breathe with the mask on, air passes through five important layers of protection before it reaches your lungs. Each layer has a different density in order to filter particles of different sizes.

MINIMUM AIRLEAK

To further improve the fit of the mask and stop air from sneaking in around the edges, each filter is equipped with a 3D memory nose-foam and an adjustable nose clip that gives the mask minimal air leakage, regardless of one's face shape.

MAXIMUM JOY

Velcro800 creates no interruption. Whether it's a cigarette break, your favourite pepepro flavour, or that quick sip of coke you just got from McDonald's. Have you tried Maki?
